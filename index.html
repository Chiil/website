Surface-atmosphere-cloud interactions
and atmospheric dynamics

Our group at Wageningen University studies the fast interactions between the land, sea, and ice surface, the atmospheric boundary layer and clouds, and their role in the large-scale atmospheric circulation.

Research

The research in our group is organized in three-dimensional simulation with MicroHH, interpretation of observations and climate modeling.
